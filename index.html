Color Guide

Here at HouseStore, we take color seriously. Below, we'll explore each of our base colors, and we'll explain how different
tints and shades of that color can take your interiors and exteriors to the next level!

 
 
 

Red

Firetrucks, lipstick, fresh berries – red is a color
with power, emotion, intensity. For this reason we reccommend using red as accent colors. Red is particularly suited to kitchens, as it is said to evoke hunger!

Green

They say geniuses choose green – we think any of these verdant colors will look smart! Deck your halls like a lush outdoor space, adorn your rooms withemerald, or just make you houseguest green with envy!

Blue

Blues don't give us the blues – in fact, blue is one of the most popular colors in home interiors, and for good reason! Energizing like a summer sky, claming like a lakeshore, light as mist or deep and indigo, blue can do it all.
